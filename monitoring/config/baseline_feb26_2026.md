# BRACE4PEACE Threat Baseline — February 26, 2026

This baseline anchors the monitoring system. Alerts are only issued for genuinely NEW intelligence beyond what is documented here.

---

## South Sudan — CRITICAL

- **Jonglei crisis**: 280,000+ displaced since December 2025 (rising to 638,600 total IDPs in Jonglei by Feb 10)
- **Johnson Olony**: Recorded instructing forces to "spare no lives" — later apologized (Feb 5) after backlash
- **Machar trial**: First VP Dr. Riek Machar under house arrest since March 2025; trial ongoing for treason, crimes against humanity. Adjourned to March 2, 2026
- **Terekeka**: 27 killed in violence
- **Cyber Crimes Act 2026**: Signed into law, aimed at curbing online hate speech
- **OHCHR**: 189 civilians killed in January 2026 alone; rights violations surged 45% month-over-month
- **Airstrikes**: Government using aerial bombardments including incendiary bombs in Jonglei
- **UNMISS/CHRSS**: Joint warning on incitement and command failures risking mass atrocities (Jan 25)
- **Operation Enduring Peace**: SSPDF announced major offensive in Jonglei (Jan 28)

## Kenya — HIGH

- **Hate speech cases**: 12 under investigation (IG Kanja, Feb 25)
- **Linda Mwananchi**: Violence incidents
- **2027 election**: Mobilization and tribal rhetoric emerging
- **Al-Shabaab**: Ramadan plot foiled in Kenya
- **NCIC**: Under criticism for inactivity (MPs questioning effectiveness, Feb 28)

## Somalia — HIGH

- **Al-Shabaab**: Stable-high threat level; NISA killed 23+ in raids
- **Ramadan**: Al-Shabaab executions reported
- **Turkey-Somalia**: Disinformation campaign
- **Clan discourse**: Ongoing ethnic/clan tensions
- **AFRICOM strikes**: Dramatically escalated — 26 strikes in January 2026 alone (12x increase over 2024)

## Al-Shabaab Media — HIGH

- **Shahada News Agency**: Expanding international coverage; claiming attacks
- **Al-Kataib Media**: Producing attack videos (e.g., Middle Shabelle operation)
- **Radio Andalus**: Active broadcasting
- **Telegram**: Primary recruitment and propaganda platform
- **Somalia government**: Banned Telegram/TikTok (Aug 2023), criminalized publishing AS content
